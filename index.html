Frequently Asked Questions

Is my laptop compatible with Linux?
Most modern laptops are compatible with Linux. We will check your laptop's specifications before installation.

Can I still use Windows after installation?
Yes, if you opt for a dual-boot setup, you can choose between Linux and Windows when your laptop starts.

What happens to my files?
Your files will remain safe. If you're using dual boot, both operating systems will share the data partitions.

What Linux distribution should I choose?
We recommend Ubuntu for beginners, but we can help you choose based on your needs.
